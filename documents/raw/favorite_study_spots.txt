[POST TITLE] Favorite Study Spots

[POST BODY]
I'm a graduating senior and it took me a while to find some good study spaces at GW so here's my list for anyone who might need it! Things I personally look for in a study space: quiet, not crowded, natural light/soft light. I also mainly study in the mornings and mid-day so these spaces may be busy in the afternoon/evenings. Gelman Floor 3 (specifically the private nooks completely on the right in front of the windows AND the computer lab which I've had all to myself many times) Gelman Floor 2 Lounge (always find space here) Milken (currently Floors 2-4 are open to all students and it's never busy in the mornings) Elliot Floor 3 classrooms (this only works during finals once there's no more classes, a lot of natural light and a big space all to yourself) SEH First Floor and Basement (empty in the mornings) Yours Truly Hotel Lobby (this is super loud but you can find some quieter corners) Capital One Café (generally quiet second floor and good hot chocolate) West End Public Library (can be hit or miss)

Adding some of my favorite on campus spots: Philips basement and couches on the 6th floor (really great if you like a little bit of background noise, lots of piano playing in the basement) District House B2 (great if you need outlets and somewhere in the middle of campus) Any dorm roof (this is great during nice weather months, in my experience never crowded)
